BetSmart Pro: Dual-Engine Soccer Analytics & ZAR Calculator
BetSmart Pro is a high-performance, data-driven dashboard designed specifically for South African soccer bettors who want to move away from "gut feelings" and start using professional-grade mathematics. By combining real-time Premier League data with an advanced Poisson probability engine, this app identifies value where bookmakers often miss it.

🚀 Key Features
1. Dual-API Analytics Engine
The app syncs directly with international football databases (Football-Data.org and API-Sports) to pull live league standings and upcoming fixtures. It ensures your data is always fresh, accurate, and ready for the week's action.

2. Automated "Value" Picker
Forget manual research. The engine runs a Poisson Distribution model on every upcoming match, comparing team strengths, defensive gaps, and historical scoring averages.

Confidence Scoring: Every team is assigned a percentage-based win probability.

The Top 4: The app automatically flags the four strongest statistical "Value" picks of the week.

3. ZAR System 3/4 "Safety Net" Calculator
Built specifically for the South African market (ZAR), this specialized calculator helps you build System 3/4 Accumulators.

Risk Mitigation: Instead of an "all-or-nothing" multi-bet, the calculator splits your stake across four combinations (Trebles).

Safety Net: It tells you exactly how much you will profit even if one team lets you down, ensuring you keep your capital safe while chasing high payouts.

4. Professional Visual Interface
Live Team Logos: Automatically fetches official club crests for a clean, professional look.

Mobile Optimized: Designed to be "installed" on Android home screens for quick access at the stadium or on the go.

Dark Mode UI: A sleek, high-contrast dashboard that makes reading odds and percentages effortless.

💰 The Strategy
"Profit through Probability, not Luck."

BetSmart Pro isn't just about picking winners; it’s about managing your money like a professional. By using the app to identify High Confidence games and then applying the System 3/4 Calculator, you create a mathematical "safety net" that protects your bankroll and turns long-term betting into a structured financial strategy.
